About HCL Restaurant

Our Story

HCL Restaurant was established in 2014 in the beautiful city of Rome, Italy. From our humble beginnings, we have grown to serve authentic international cuisine with a focus on quality, taste, and excellence.

Branches

Our passion for fine dining has taken us beyond Italy. Today, we proudly operate branches in:

  • France
  • Spain
  • Poland
  • Austria

Working Hours & Shifts

We ensure our staff works efficiently in shifts to provide you the best service:

  • Morning Shift: 08:00 AM – 03:00 PM
  • Night Shift: 04:00 PM – 11:00 PM

Our Rules & Philosophy

At HCL Restaurant, we follow strict hygiene and service standards. Our staff is trained to maintain a welcoming atmosphere, providing excellent service while respecting both customers and colleagues. Our philosophy is simple: delicious food, happy customers!
