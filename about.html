About HCl Restaurant

HCl Restaurant was founded in 2014 in Rome, Italy, with a mission to bring authentic Italian flavors to the world. Our restaurant blends traditional recipes with modern hospitality for an unforgettable dining experience.

Our Specialty & Team

We focus on signature pasta & pizzas, crafted by our expert chefs. Our dedicated team ensures top-notch hospitality and service to make every guest feel special.
